Vaishno Devi

Located in the Trikuta Mountains of Jammu & Kashmir, Vaishno Devi is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites in India, attracting millions of devotees every year. The temple is dedicated to Mata Vaishno Devi, and reaching the shrine requires a 13 km trek from Katra, with options for ponies, palkis, and helicopter services. The best time to visit is March to October, though it remains open year-round. The nearest airport is Jammu (50 km), and the nearest railway station is Katra. A must-visit for spiritual seekers!
