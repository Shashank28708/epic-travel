Auli

Auli is a stunning hill station and a top skiing destination in India. Surrounded by snow-capped peaks and lush meadows, it offers breathtaking views of Nanda Devi and Mana Parvat. Best visited from December to March for skiing or April to June for trekking and sightseeing. Reach via Joshimath (16 km) by cable car or road, with the nearest airport in Dehradun (279 km). Perfect for adventure lovers, Auli is a must-visit for its pristine beauty and thrilling activities.
