Munnar

Nestled in the Western Ghats of Kerala, Munnar is a picturesque hill station known for its rolling tea plantations, misty hills, and waterfalls. A perfect retreat for nature lovers and honeymooners, it offers attractions like Eravikulam National Park, Mattupetty Dam, and Top Station. Best visited from September to May, it provides a cool and refreshing escape.
