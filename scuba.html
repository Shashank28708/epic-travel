Scuba Diving

Dive into the mesmerizing world beneath the waves with scuba diving, an adventure that lets you explore vibrant coral reefs, exotic marine life, and mysterious shipwrecks. Top destinations in India include Andaman & Nicobar Islands (Havelock, Neil Island), Goa, Lakshadweep, and Netrani Island (Karnataka). The best time for scuba diving is October to May, when waters are clear and calm. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, certified instructors ensure a safe and unforgettable underwater experience.
