Spiti valley

A high-altitude cold desert in Himachal Pradesh, Spiti Valley is known for its stunning landscapes, ancient monasteries, and Tibetan culture. The best time to visit is May to September when roads are open and weather is pleasant. Reach via Manali (200 km) or Shimla (450 km) by road. Ideal for adventure seekers, Spiti offers trekking, camping, and breathtaking views.
