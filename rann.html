Rann Of Kutch

A vast salt marsh in Gujarat, the Rann of Kutch is famous for its surreal white landscapes, vibrant culture, and the annual Rann Utsav festival. Best visited from November to February, it offers mesmerizing full-moon nights, camel rides, and cultural performances.
