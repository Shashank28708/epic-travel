Chandra Taal

Located in Himachal Pradesh at 4,300 meters, Chandra Taal is a breathtaking high-altitude lake known for its crystal-clear waters that change colors with the sky. Surrounded by the Himalayas and barren Spiti landscapes, it's a paradise for trekkers and nature lovers. Best visited from June to September, as it remains snow-covered in winter. Reach via Manali or Kaza, with the last motorable point at Batal (14 km), followed by a trek. Ideal for camping and stargazing, Chandra Taal offers an unforgettable experience.
