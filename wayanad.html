wayanad

Nestled in the Western Ghats, Wayanad is a lush green paradise known for its misty hills, waterfalls, wildlife, and spice plantations. A perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure seekers, top attractions include Edakkal Caves, Banasura Sagar Dam, Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary, and Soochipara Falls. Best visited from October to May, it offers trekking, wildlife safaris, and serene getaways. Well-connected via Kozhikode (85 km), Wayanad is an unmissable destination for those seeking peace and adventure.
