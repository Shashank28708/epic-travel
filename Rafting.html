Rafting

Rafting is an adrenaline-pumping adventure sport that lets you navigate through thrilling river rapids while enjoying stunning natural surroundings. Popular destinations in India include Rishikesh (Ganges), Zanskar River (Ladakh), Teesta River (Sikkim), and Beas River (Himachal Pradesh). The best time for rafting is September to June, depending on the location and river conditions. Whether you're a beginner or an expert, guided tours ensure a safe yet exhilarating experience. Perfect for adventure lovers, rafting offers the ultimate water adventure.
