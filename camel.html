Camel Safari

Embark on an unforgettable camel safari, exploring vast desert landscapes, golden sand dunes, and rich cultural heritage. Popular in Rajasthan (Jaisalmer, Bikaner, Pushkar) and Ladakh’s Nubra Valley, this adventure offers a glimpse into the traditional desert lifestyle. Best experienced from October to March, camel safaris range from short rides to multi-day expeditions with camping under starry skies. Ideal for travelers seeking a unique and immersive desert experience, it’s a must-try adventure in India.
