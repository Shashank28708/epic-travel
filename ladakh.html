Leh Ladakh

LEH LADAKH, IN NORTHERN INDIA, IS KNOWN FOR ITS STUNNING LANDSCAPES, ANCIENT MONASTERIES, AND HIGH-ALTITUDE ADVENTURES. TOP ATTRACTIONS INCLUDE PANGONG LAKE, NUBRA VALLEY, KHARDUNG LA PASS, AND THIKSEY MONASTERY. THE BEST TIME TO VISIT IS FROM MAY TO SEPTEMBER. POPULAR ACTIVITIES INCLUDE TREKKING, BIKING, CAMPING, AND EXPLORING LADAKHI CULTURE. REACH LEH BY AIR OR TAKE A SCENIC ROAD TRIP VIA MANALI OR SRINAGAR.
