Darjeeling

Perched in the Himalayas of West Bengal, Darjeeling is famed for its tea gardens, panoramic mountain views, and colonial charm. A must-visit for nature lovers, it offers attractions like the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train), Tiger Hill (for sunrise views of Kanchenjunga), Batasia Loop, and Peace Pagoda. The best time to visit is March to May and September to November. Easily accessible via Bagdogra Airport (70 km) and New Jalpaiguri Railway Station (62 km), Darjeeling is perfect for a refreshing hill getaway.
